Noise in the Census

The 2020 Census is fast approaching.

The 2020 Census is fast approaching. We will be collecting household data from everyone in the country to paint an accurate picture of life in the United States. These data are only collected once every 10 years, and are used for deciding how many representatives that each state will have in the US Congress.

It is the top priority of the U.S. Census Bureau protect the privacy of the data that you entrust to us. One our primary methods of protecting data privacy is by adding ‘noise’ to the data, which makes it difficult to use the data to discover individual people’s data from the aggregate statistics we release.
